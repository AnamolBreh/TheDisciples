About Gamemodes

We gonna have 3 Gamemodes in starting that would be:

1. Survival

In this Gamemode, there would be simple gameplay. But there would be a lot of features like economy, Custom enchantments etc.

2. Bedwars

As most of you would be aware about what Bedwars is. Here you have to break other players bed and survive till last.

3. Lifesteal

This Gamemode is similar to Survival but here when you die you looses your heart and as soon as you loose your all hearts then you can't join gamemode again but you can gain more hearts by stealing or killing other players.

Our Support Team

We try to provide best Support to you so we have following team

Helpers

So, Basically helpers helps you at any case you are stuck. They show you right way of doing things and can mute anyone if he/she break rules.

Moderators

So, Basically Moderators or Mod are the one who helps players as well as helps to maintain server. They have powers to kick and ban members. In mod category we have "Jr.Mod" and "Mod".

Admins

So, Basically Admins are the 2nd top most category of staff team. They manage servers and have all access of all things except some critical things that only founder can have.
