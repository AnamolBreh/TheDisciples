Our server's Rules & Regulations

Aiming at creating an atmosphere and environment of brotherhood, we wish you a happy journey on our server.

Discord Rules and Guidelines:

1) • Discord TOS:
↦ Please respect and follow everything put under the Discord Terms of Service and Communit Guidelines. Violation of these Ter will result in a ban from this server.

2) • Appropriate behaviour:
↦ Under no circumstances, do we allow NSFW content or discussion of self-harm. Please do not discuss controversial topics such as religion or politics.

↦ All players have the right to be spoken to or addressed in a respectful manner. Everyone's opinion should also be respected. Everyone should be treated equally.

↦ Any sort of discrimination or hateful behaviors towards anybody will not be tolerated.

↦ English is the language of the server and we allow use of it only.

↦ Mentioning hack clients in chat is not allowed.

↦ Swearing/Spamming is not allowed, any form of Swearing/Spamming including videos, gif's or emoji is not allowed.

3) • Channel rules:

↦ When posting/chatting in a channel, make sure your post/message is related and addreses to the rules of the channel. This Includes talking in a voice channel. Rules of the channels can be found in the discription of the channel.

4) • Respectful behaviour:

↦ Respect our staff members. Give them time and don't be impatient! Do not ping staff without a reason (especially owners). Ghost pinging is not allowed and may lead to permanent mute/ban.

5) • Advertising:

↦ Advertising in DM's is not allowed and multiple instances can get you banned. Any proof in DM's is none of our problems unless stated he or she publicizes here with concrete evidence of IP.

↦ It is acceptable to use the names of other servers, such as hypixle , purple prison, etc., but it is not acceptable to use those names to promote your server, such as by saying "join serverxyz" and it is also not acceptable to use any other server's IP address than the ones. listed in the "?ip" command doing so will lead you to a mute/ban.

6) • Punishment evading:

↦ Using alts or any other method to bypass/evade a given Punishment is not allowed. using the minecraft-discord chat channels while being muted ingame will be considered as Punishment evading.

7) • Racism:

↦ Racism against ANY group of people is not allowed. Doing so will result in a ban.

8) • Impersonation:

↦ Impersonation of any staff member is not allowed. Anyone caught doing this will be punished.

9) • Member safety:

↦ Sharing ANY type of phishing link, IP grabber or malicious link will result in a permanent ban, the server is supposed to be a safe and secure environment for everyone.

10) • Auto mod:

↦ Trying to bypass the Auto mod in any manner is not allowed, doing so can lead you to a mute/ban.

Note:

↦ Staff has every right to punish you if found doing anything offensive other than rules stated above. If you feel you are innocent then create a ticket.

Have fun! Try your best to contribute to our community.
